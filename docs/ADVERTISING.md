# Advertising, Sponsorship, and Affiliate Disclosure / 广告、赞助与推广披露

Last updated / 最后更新：2026-07-14

## Current project status / 当前项目状态

The MemoArk-maintained repository and demo data currently contain no paid
advertising, paid placement, affiliate links, or contributor-operated ad or
analytics tracking. Companies that sponsored the upstream Memos project are
not automatically sponsors of MemoArk, and MemoArk does not claim or receive
their upstream sponsorship.

MemoArk 项目维护的仓库和演示数据目前不包含付费广告、付费展示、推广返佣链接，也不包含
由项目贡献者运营的广告或分析追踪。赞助上游 Memos 项目的公司不会因此自动成为 MemoArk
的赞助方，MemoArk 不会声称或接收这些上游赞助。

This statement covers the MemoArk-maintained distribution. A self-hosted
instance operator may publish independent content or add integrations; those
choices do not represent an endorsement by the MemoArk project.

以上说明适用于 MemoArk 项目维护的发行版。自托管实例运营者可以自行发布内容或添加集成；
这些行为不代表 MemoArk 项目提供背书。

## Required point-of-display disclosure / 必须就地标注

Any future paid, sponsored, or affiliate content maintained by the MemoArk
project must be clearly labelled **where the content appears**, in language the
audience can understand. A central policy page or changelog entry alone is not
enough.

MemoArk 项目未来维护的任何付费、赞助或推广返佣内容，都必须在**内容实际展示的位置**使用
受众能够理解的语言清楚标注。只在统一政策页面或更新日志中说明不够。

The nearby disclosure must identify, as applicable:

- that the content is an advertisement, sponsorship, paid placement, or
  affiliate link;
- the paying or sponsoring party;
- who receives money, services, discounts, commission, or another material
  benefit;
- whether the link or placement uses tracking parameters, cookies, pixels, or
  sends data to another party;
- whether compensation affected ranking, placement, or editorial selection.

就地披露应根据实际情况说明：

- 内容属于广告、赞助、付费展示还是推广返佣链接；
- 付款方或赞助方是谁；
- 谁会获得资金、服务、折扣、佣金或其他实质利益；
- 链接或展示是否使用追踪参数、Cookie、追踪像素，或向其他主体发送数据；
- 报酬是否影响排序、展示位置或内容选择。

Labels must remain visible without requiring a user to inspect source code,
hover over a link, or search another document. Examples include
"Sponsored / 赞助", "Advertisement / 广告", and
"Affiliate link - commission may be earned / 推广链接 - 可能获得佣金".

标注必须直接可见，不能要求用户查看源代码、悬停链接或另行搜索文档。例如可使用
“Sponsored / 赞助”“Advertisement / 广告”或
“Affiliate link - commission may be earned / 推广链接 - 可能获得佣金”。

## Project-wide record / 项目统一记录

When a paid relationship exists, this file must also record the sponsor or
advertiser, beneficiary, relationship type, affected surfaces, start and end
dates, and tracking behavior. This central record supplements the point-of-
display label; it does not replace it.

存在付费关系时，还必须在本文记录赞助方或广告主、受益方、关系类型、影响范围、起止日期
和追踪方式。统一记录只是就地标注的补充，不能替代就地标注。

**Current paid relationships / 当前付费关系：None / 无。**

## Reporting an undisclosed placement / 报告未披露内容

Report a suspected undisclosed commercial placement through the project's
[issue tracker](https://github.com/harrychin-cn/memoark/issues). Include the
page, screenshot or content reference, and the link involved.

如发现疑似未披露的商业展示，请通过项目
[Issue](https://github.com/harrychin-cn/memoark/issues) 报告，并附上页面、截图或内容位置
以及相关链接。
